## Getting Started

### Download the App

**Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "MedhaOS Patient"
3. Tap "Install"

**iOS:**
1. Open App Store
2. Search for "MedhaOS Patient"
3. Tap "Get"

### Create Your Account

1. Open the MedhaOS app
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter your mobile number
4. Verify with OTP
5. Complete your profile:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Gender
   - Preferred language
   - ABHA ID (if you have one)

### Link Your ABHA ID

ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is your unique health ID that stores all your medical records.

**If you have an ABHA ID:**
1. Go to Profile → Link ABHA ID
2. Enter your 14-digit ABHA number
3. Verify with OTP
4. Your health records will be automatically imported

**If you don't have an ABHA ID:**
1. Go to Profile → Create ABHA ID
2. Follow the on-screen instructions
3. Verify with Aadhaar OTP
4. Your ABHA ID will be created instantly

## Voice-Based Triage

### What is Triage?

Triage helps us understand how urgent your medical condition is and connects you with the right doctor at the right facility.

### How to Use Voice Triage

1. **Tap the Microphone Button** on the home screen
   - The large blue button in the center

2. **Select Your Language**
   - Choose from Hindi, English, Tamil, Kannada, and 18+ other languages
   - You can speak in your native language!

3. **Describe Your Symptoms**
   - Speak clearly into your phone
   - Tell us what's bothering you
   - Example: "मुझे सीने में दर्द है" (I have chest pain)

4. **Answer Follow-up Questions**
   - The AI will ask 5-7 questions to understand your condition better
   - You can answer by voice or by tapping options
   - Questions may include:
     - When did symptoms start?
     - How severe is the pain?
     - Any other symptoms?

5. **View Your Urgency Score**
   - You'll receive a score from 0-100
   - **Red (70-100):** High urgency - Visit Emergency Department
   - **Yellow (40-69):** Moderate - Schedule OPD appointment
   - **Green (0-39):** Low - Home care or routine checkup

6. **Get Recommendations**
   - The app will suggest:
     - Nearest hospital
     - Appropriate specialist
     - Estimated wait time
     - Navigation directions

### Tips for Better Results

✅ **Do:**
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Mention all symptoms
- Be honest about severity
- Include relevant medical history

❌ **Don't:**
- Rush through questions
- Skip important details
- Ignore follow-up questions

## Booking Appointments

### Book an Appointment

1. **From Triage Results:**
   - After triage, tap "Confirm Appointment"
   - The system automatically selects the best facility and doctor

2. **Manual Booking:**
   - Tap "Book Appointment" on home screen
   - Select specialty (Cardiology, Orthopedics, etc.)
   - Choose hospital
   - Select doctor
   - Pick date and time
   - Confirm booking

### View Your Appointments

1. Go to "Appointments" tab
2. See upcoming appointments with:
   - Date and time
   - Doctor name and specialty
   - Hospital location
   - Queue position
   - Estimated wait time

### Navigate to Hospital

1. Open your appointment
2. Tap "Navigate"
3. Choose your preferred map app (Google Maps, etc.)
4. Follow directions

### Reschedule or Cancel

1. Open the appointment
2. Tap "Reschedule" or "Cancel"
3. Select new date/time (for reschedule)
4. Confirm changes

### Join Virtual Queue

For OPD appointments:
1. Arrive at hospital
2. Open appointment in app
3. Tap "I'm Here"
4. Your queue position updates in real-time
5. Get notified when it's your turn

## Managing Medications

### View Medications

1. Go to "Medications" tab
2. See all your current medications:
   - Medicine name
   - Dosage
   - Frequency
   - Time to take
   - Remaining doses

### Set Reminders

Reminders are automatically set when your doctor prescribes medication.

**To customize:**
1. Tap on a medication
2. Tap "Edit Reminder"
3. Set preferred time
4. Choose notification sound
5. Save changes

### Mark as Taken

When you take your medicine:
1. Tap the notification
2. Tap "Mark as Taken"
3. Or swipe right on the medication card

### Verify Your Medicine

Use the camera to verify you're taking the correct medicine:

1. Tap "Scan Medicine"
2. Point camera at the pill/tablet
3. The app will identify it
4. Confirm it matches your prescription
5. Get alerts if there's a mismatch

### Request Refill

When running low:
1. Tap on medication
2. Tap "Request Refill"
3. Choose pharmacy
4. Select pickup or delivery
5. Complete payment

### Access Your Records

1. Go to "Records" tab
2. View all your medical information:
   - Medical history
   - Diagnostic reports
   - Prescriptions
   - Vaccination records
   - Allergies

### View Diagnostic Reports

1. Tap "Diagnostic Reports"

### Upload Documents

1. Tap "Upload Document"
2. Choose source:
   - Take photo
   - Select from gallery
   - Scan document
3. Add details:
   - Document type
   - Date
   - Notes
4. Save

## Emergency Features

### Emergency Button

The red "Emergency" button is always visible on your home screen.

**When to use:**
- Severe chest pain
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe bleeding
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe allergic reaction

**What happens:**
1. Tap "Emergency"
2. Your location is shared
3. Nearest emergency services notified
4. Ambulance dispatched
5. Your emergency contacts alerted
6. Your medical history sent to hospital

### Emergency Contacts

Set up emergency contacts:
1. Go to Profile → Emergency Contacts
2. Add up to 3 contacts
3. They'll be notified in emergencies

### Medical Alert Card

Create a digital medical alert card:
1. Go to Profile → Medical Alert
2. Add critical information:
   - Allergies
   - Chronic conditions
   - Current medications
   - Blood type
3. Show to first responders in emergency

## Settings & Privacy

### Language Settings

1. Go to Profile → Settings → Language
2. Select your preferred language
3. App interface and voice will change

### Notification Settings

1. Go to Settings → Notifications
2. Customize:
   - Medication reminders
   - Appointment reminders
   - Health tips
   - Emergency alerts

### Privacy Settings

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Control:
   - Who can see your records
   - Data sharing preferences
   - Location tracking
   - Biometric authentication

### Biometric Login

Enable fingerprint or face recognition:
1. Go to Settings → Security
2. Enable "Biometric Login"
3. Follow setup instructions

### Data Download

Download all your data:
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Tap "Download My Data"
3. Receive email with download link
4. Data in FHIR format

### Delete Account

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Tap "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data will be permanently deleted
